An e-commerce store is an online platform that allows businesses to sell products or services over the internet. Customers can browse items, compare prices, add products to a cart, and complete purchases without visiting a physical shop. It operates 24/7 and can reach customers globally.it allows customers to browse products, compare prices, add items to a cart, and complete purchases using secure online payment methods. The store operates digitally, removing the need for a physical location and enabling shopping anytime and anywhere.
It typically includes product listings with images and descriptions, search and filter options, a shopping cart system, secure checkout, multiple payment options, order tracking, and customer accounts. Many e-commerce stores also offer reviews, discounts, customer support, and delivery management to improve user experience.

 features:

1.Product Catalog: Displays products with images, descriptions, prices, and availability. Items are organized into categories for easy browsing.

2.Search & Filters: Helps users quickly find products using keywords, categories, price ranges, or ratings.

3. Shopping Cart: Allows customers to add, remove, or update items before purchasing.
   
4.Secure Checkout: Ensures safe transactions with encryption and secure payment gateways.

5. Multiple Payment Options: Supports credit/debit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets, and sometimes cash on delivery.
   
6. User Accounts:  Customers can create accounts to save personal details, track orders, and view purchase history.
   
7.Order Management: Tracks orders from placement to delivery, including shipping status and notifications.

8. Reviews & Ratings:Customers can share feedback, helping others make informed decisions.
